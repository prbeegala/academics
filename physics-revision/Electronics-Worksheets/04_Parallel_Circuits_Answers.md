### Marking tips
- Insist on **units** in numerical answers.
- For Q9, full marks only if the pupil's mistake is identified **and** the correct rule (I = V/R per branch) is stated.
- For Q5 and Q6, look for both the **outcome** and the **reason** for full marks.

### Common slip-ups
- Treating parallel resistance like series resistance and **adding** the ohms — wrong! Parallel total is always less than the smallest.
- Forgetting that the voltage on **every** branch is the full source voltage.
- Saying "if one branch breaks, the whole circuit stops" — that's series, not parallel.
- Mixing up where the current "splits": it splits **at the junction**, not at the cell.
