# Srita's KS2 SATs Practice Pack

Year 6 SATs preparation pack — focused practice for the week before SATs.

## Focus areas (per Srita)
- **Heavy focus:** Maths Reasoning, SPAG (Grammar, Punctuation & Spelling)
- **Light revision:** Maths Arithmetic, Reading Comprehension

## Suggested 5-day schedule

| Day | Morning (45 min) | Afternoon (30 min) |
|-----|------------------|---------------------|
| Mon | SPAG Paper 1 (Grammar & Punctuation) | SPAG Revision Sheet — read through |
| Tue | Maths Reasoning Paper A | Reasoning Revision Sheet — strategies |
| Wed | SPAG Paper 2 (Spelling, dictated by adult) + Reading Comprehension | Review wrong answers from Mon/Tue |
| Thu | Maths Reasoning Paper B | Maths Arithmetic Paper |
| Fri | Mixed Mini-Mock (timed, mixed questions) | Light review — flashcards / favourite topics |

## Folder layout
- `Papers/` — full mock papers
- `RevisionSheets/` — quick-reference revision sheets
- `AnswerKeys/` — answers and worked solutions

## Tips for exam week
- Sleep early; do not cram the night before.
- Read every question **twice** in Reasoning papers — many marks are lost to misreading.
- In SPAG, check whether the question says "tick **one** box" or "tick **all** that apply".
- Show working in Reasoning — method marks count even if the final answer is wrong.

Good luck, Srita! 🌟
