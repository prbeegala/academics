## Strategic Takeaways

1. **Geometry is king** — consistently ~25% of every paper and dominates the hard questions. Angles and area are the top subtopics.
2. **Algebra is rising fast** — jumped from 14% to 21%. "Forming equations from word problems" and simultaneous equations dominate, especially in hard questions.
3. **Arithmetic is easy marks** — still ~20% of the paper but almost entirely in Q1–8. Focus on fractions, BODMAS, and percentages for quick wins.
4. **Number Theory spans all difficulties** — primes, factors, divisibility, and digit puzzles appear at every level.
5. **Combinatorics is rare but almost always hard** — 56% of combinatorics questions are in Q18–25.
6. **Logic has faded** — used to appear 2–3 times per paper, now appears sporadically.

### Recommended Preparation Priority

1. Geometry (angles + area)
2. Algebra (word problems → equations)
3. Number Theory (primes, factors, remainders)
4. Arithmetic (fractions, BODMAS)
5. Combinatorics (counting)
