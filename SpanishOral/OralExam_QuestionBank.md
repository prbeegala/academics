## ⚡ How to Use This

- **Read** each answer aloud twice.
- **Highlight** opinion phrases (in **bold**) – examiners love them.
- **Mix tenses** – present, near future (*voy a*), preterite (*fui*), future (*iré*), conditional (*me gustaría*).
- If you forget a word, say *"¿cómo se dice…?"* or describe it – **never** switch to English.

### 🌟 Top "wow" phrases to drop in

| Spanish | English |
|---|---|
| **En mi opinión…** | In my opinion… |
| **Creo que…** | I think that… |
| **Lo bueno / lo malo es que…** | The good / bad thing is that… |
| **Lo que más me gusta es…** | What I like most is… |
| **Aunque…, también…** | Although…, also… |
| **Sin embargo…** | However… |
| **Por ejemplo…** | For example… |
| **Me gustaría + infinitive** | I would like to… |

**🇪🇸** Cuando hace sol, **me encanta** ir al parque, montar en bicicleta o tomar un helado con mis amigas. Cuando llueve, prefiero quedarme en casa: leo un libro, veo una película o cocino un pastel con mi madre. **En mi opinión**, no hay tiempo malo, sólo planes diferentes.

**🇬🇧** When it's sunny, **I love** going to the park, riding my bike or having an ice-cream with my friends. When it rains, I prefer to stay at home: I read a book, watch a film or bake a cake with my mum. **In my opinion**, there is no bad weather, just different plans.

In the summer **we're going to** travel to [✏️ destination] for two weeks. **I'm very excited** because it's going to be a fantastic holiday.
