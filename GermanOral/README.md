4. **Practise interruptions** with a parent using `07_FollowUpBank.md`.
5. **Mock test** with `08_PracticeScript_5min.md` — time it, aim for 5 minutes total.

## Exam-day golden rules ✨

1. **Slow down.** Speaking slowly = clearer = sounds more confident.
2. **Use connectives.** *weil, aber, obwohl, deshalb, und dann* — they impress examiners.
3. **Always give an opinion + a reason.** *Ich finde X interessant, weil…*
4. **Don't panic if interrupted.** Take a breath, answer the new question, then continue.
5. **Use a perfect tense once** somewhere (e.g., the restaurant question is perfect for that).

### 🆘 Rescue phrases (memorise these!)

| German | English |
|---|---|
| **Können Sie das bitte wiederholen?** | Could you repeat that please? |
| **Können Sie bitte langsamer sprechen?** | Could you speak more slowly please? |
| **Wie sagt man "X" auf Deutsch?** | How do you say "X" in German? |
| **Moment mal…** | One moment… (great filler) |
| **Das ist eine gute Frage.** | That's a good question. (buys thinking time) |
| **Ich bin nicht sicher, aber ich glaube…** | I'm not sure, but I think… |
| **Entschuldigung, ich habe das nicht verstanden.** | Sorry, I didn't understand that. |

> 💡 **Never say "ähhh" for too long.** Use *"Also…"* or *"Naja…"* — they sound German and natural.

## What examiners reward at A2

- ✅ Variety of tenses (present + at least one perfect)
- ✅ Opinions with reasons (*weil + verb at the end*)
- ✅ Connectives (und, aber, oder, weil, obwohl, deshalb, dann)
- ✅ Time/frequency words (oft, manchmal, jeden Tag, am Wochenende)
- ✅ Recovery from mistakes (don't freeze — keep talking!)
