### Q17. Circle theorems **[5 marks]**

- M1: Use alternate segment theorem with tangent and chord AC.
- A1: angle ABC = **47°**.
- A1: angle ACB = **90°** because AB is a diameter and angle in a semicircle is 90°.
- A1: angle BAC = 180° − 47° − 90° = **43°**.
- A1: angle ADC = 180° − angle ABC = 180° − 47° = **133°**, using opposite angles in a cyclic quadrilateral.
