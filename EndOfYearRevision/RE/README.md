## 📝 Exam Essay Structure (use this every time!)

- **Introduction** – State your view and the **main reason** behind it.
- **Main Body** – 2–3 **PEE** paragraphs:
  - **P**oint
  - **E**vidence (use the sources!)
  - **E**xplanation (link back to the question)
- **Counter-argument** – *“On the other hand, some might argue…”*
- **Conclusion** – Restate your view, explain *why* you arrived at it.

## ✅ Final Tips for the Exam

- **Use names, dates and quotes** wherever you can — examiners love specifics.
- **Always answer the question that was asked**, not the one you wish was asked.
- **Plan longer answers** with quick PEE bullets before writing.
- **Argue both sides**, then come down on one.
- **Spell key religious terms correctly** — *Eucharist, Resurrection, Hijra, Sanhedrin, Pharisee, transubstantiation*.
- 🎯 Read each question **twice** before starting!
