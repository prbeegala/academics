### 🔑 Key Ideas

- Length: 10 mm = 1 cm, 100 cm = 1 m, 1000 m = 1 km
- Mass: 1000 mg = 1 g, 1000 g = 1 kg, 1000 kg = 1 tonne
- Capacity: 1000 ml = 1 litre
- Volume and capacity: 1 cm³ = 1 ml, so 1000 cm³ = 1 litre
- 1 m³ = 1000 litres
- Area units are squared: 1 m = 100 cm, so 1 m² = 100 × 100 = 10 000 cm² (NOT 100 cm²)
- Also: 1 cm² = 100 mm², so 1 mm² = 0.01 cm²
- Volume units are cubed: 1 m = 100 cm, so 1 m³ = 100 × 100 × 100 = 1 000 000 cm³
- Time: 1 hour = 60 minutes = 3600 seconds. To change minutes to decimal hours, divide by 60.

---

### ✏️ Worked Examples

**Example 1.** Convert 2.5 m² to cm².

1 m² = 10 000 cm²  
2.5 m² = 2.5 × 10 000 = 25 000 cm²

**Example 2.** A container holds 0.36 m³ of water. How many litres is this?

1 m³ = 1000 litres  
0.36 m³ = 0.36 × 1000 = 360 litres
