### 🔑 Key Ideas

**Speed, Distance, Time**

Formula triangle:

```text
      Distance
   Speed × Time
```

- Speed = Distance ÷ Time
- Distance = Speed × Time
- Time = Distance ÷ Speed

**Density, Mass, Volume**

Formula triangle:

```text
        Mass
   Density × Volume
```

- Density = Mass ÷ Volume
- Mass = Density × Volume
- Volume = Mass ÷ Density

**Pressure, Force, Area**

Formula triangle:

```text
        Force
   Pressure × Area
```

- Pressure = Force ÷ Area
- Force = Pressure × Area
- Area = Force ÷ Pressure

**Common unit conversions**

- 1 km = 1000 m
- 1 hour = 60 minutes = 3600 seconds
- To convert km/h to m/s: ÷ 3.6
- To convert m/s to km/h: × 3.6
- 1 g/cm³ = 1000 kg/m³
- 1 m² = 10 000 cm²
- 1 N/m² = 1 Pa

---

### ✏️ Worked Examples

**Example 1.** Convert 72 km/h to m/s.

72 km/h = 72 ÷ 3.6 m/s = 20 m/s

**Example 2.** A metal cube has mass 960 g and density 8 g/cm³. Find its volume.

Volume = Mass ÷ Density  
Volume = 960 ÷ 8 = 120 cm³
